Here are UI elements worth adding, grouped by impact:

📊 Overview / Analytics
Revenue chart — a simple bar/line chart showing weekly or monthly payout totals per venue
Booking funnel stats — Pending → Confirmed → Completed conversion rate as a visual funnel
Occupancy heatmap — a monthly grid showing how densely booked each venue is (like GitHub contribution graph)
Top performer card — "Most booked artist this month" with their avatar and stats
🏟️ Venues Tab
Venue availability badge — a pill on each venue card showing how many slots are open this week
Quick slot count — "3 available · 2 pending · 1 booked" inline stats under the venue name
Venue search/filter bar — filter cards by type (Café, Club, Resort) or capacity range
Image gallery in venue detail modal — multiple photos, not just the cover image
📅 Calendar / Slots
Slot drag-to-select — click and drag across dates to bulk-create slots
Colour-coded calendar legend — visible key for Available / Pending / Booked / Blocked colors
Slot summary sidebar — when no date is selected, show a "next 7 days" summary list instead of empty state
📋 Bookings Tab
Filter/sort bar — filter by venue, performer, or status; sort by date or budget
Bulk approve/reject — checkbox selection on pending bookings with a bulk action toolbar
Booking timeline view — a horizontal Gantt-style strip showing all confirmed gigs across venues on a weekly axis
Earnings summary card — total confirmed revenue this week / month at the top of the tab
Export button — download bookings as CSV
💬 Messages
Unread badge count on the performer list sidebar items
Message search — search across all conversations
Typing indicator (even if mock) for realism
File/image attachment button (UI only, non-functional is fine)
🔔 Notifications
Mark all as read button at the top
Notification type icons — different icons for bookings, reviews, and messages
Filter tabs — "All · Bookings · Reviews · System"
⭐ Reviews Tab
Star rating distribution bar chart — like Amazon's "43% 5-star" breakdown
Average rating badge per venue
Sentiment tags — auto-tag reviews as "Punctual", "Professional", "Great Energy" based on keywords
🎭 Performer / Artist Discovery (new tab idea)
Browse performers tab — let the org owner search and discover performers by genre, price, and radius without waiting for inbound requests
Send invite — proactively send a slot offer to a performer
🌐 Global Shell
Dark/light mode toggle in the sidebar or header
Keyboard shortcuts panel (e.g. G+V → go to Venues, G+B → Bookings)
Global search bar (Cmd+K) to jump to any venue, booking, or performer by name
Onboarding checklist — a dismissible "complete your setup" card for new org accounts showing % completion
The highest-ROI additions would be the revenue chart on the Overview tab, booking filter/sort bar, and global Cmd+K search — they make the app feel genuinely production-grade rather than a prototype.
